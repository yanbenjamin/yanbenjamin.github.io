Movies and TV Shows

Some movies and TV shows I've watched / rewatched and my (totally objective) rating out of 10. I'm broadly a fan of the science fiction, romance, and fantasy genres. I have a sweet tooth for animation, especially ones with hyper-stylized art and anything Pixar. My favorite movie of all time is Spider-Man: Into the Spider-Verse (2018).

A star (★) denotes one of my absolute favorites, whether for critical reasons, nostalgia, or vibes. One can guess what a double star means.

Also, animation is cinema. Thank you for coming to my TED talk.
