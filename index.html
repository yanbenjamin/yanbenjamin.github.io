About Me

Hi! I'm Benjamin Yan (颜斌), a student at Stanford University studying mathematics (B.S.), computer science (B.S.), and creative writing (minor). I'm fortunate to be advised by Jerry Cain and Prof. Yakov Eliashberg. I plan to graduate someday—fingers crossed.

My email is bbyan [at] stanford [dot] edu. My pronouns are he/him.

I'm broadly interested in machine learning, NLP, visual language models, and healthcare. I've previously worked at NVIDIA, Stanford AI Lab, Stanford NLP Group, and Mayo Clinic.

I'm originally from Rochester, Minnesota, where I shoveled snow, ice-skated, and admired the two hours of yearly sunshine. I'm a proud alum of Century High School and the now-closed Friedell Middle School 😢. In 2020, I was one of two Minnesotans to be named a U.S. Presidential Scholar.

Outside of work, I enjoy young adult fiction, Pixar movies (Toy Story, Ratatouille, Inside Out, Soul), hiking, being a Marvel Phase 4 evangelist, and pretending that I'm a Jedi .
