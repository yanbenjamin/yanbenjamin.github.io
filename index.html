About Me

I am a third-year student in Mathematics (BS), Computer Science (BS & MS), and Creative Writing (Minor) at Stanford University. I’m grateful to be advised by Jerry Cain and Prof. Yakov Eliashberg. In 2020, I was named a U.S. Presidential Scholar.

My research interests are broadly deep learning, self-supervised learning, and interpretable AI. I am affiliated with the Stanford AI Lab (SAIL), Stanford ML Group, Medical AI Lab at Harvard Medical School, and Stanford SNAP Group (newly!). I co-teach Stanford CS 106S, a survey course on coding for social good, and peer tutor for the Math Department.

In my free time, I enjoy queer poetry & literature, Pixar films, K-pop albums, and the wonderful world of Percy Jackson. My pronouns are he/they.
