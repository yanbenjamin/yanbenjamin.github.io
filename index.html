About Me

Hi! I'm a student at Stanford University, studying Mathematics, Creative Writing, and Computer Science. At Stanford, I teach CS 106S: Coding for Social Good; tutor for the Math Department (MATH 18-21, 51); and am involved with CS + Social Good, Math Organization, Stanford SciOly, and Golden Gate SciOly. I'm also currently a visiting research fellow at Harvard Medical School, working in the Rajpurkar Lab. This winter term ('24), I'll be at Oxford University, pursuing a tutorial in Creative Writing.
