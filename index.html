About Me

Hi! I'm a student at Stanford University. I watch animation (e.g., Spider-Verse, Gravity Falls, Spirited Away, The Owl House, Arcane, How To Train Your Dragon), read fiction, and periodically write. My pronouns are he/they. I'm American-born, of Chinese heritage, and grew up in the icy, blue-green chrysalis that is Minnesota.
