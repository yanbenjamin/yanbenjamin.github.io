About Me

Hi! I'm Benjamin Yan (颜斌), a student at Stanford University studying mathematics (B.S.), computer science (B.S.), and creative writing (minor). I'm fortunate to be advised by Jerry Cain and Prof. Yakov Eliashberg. I plan to graduate in June 2024.

My email is bbyan [at] stanford [dot] edu. My pronouns are he/him. Feel free to drop an email or contact me on my socials!

I'm broadly interested in machine learning, NLP, visual language models, and data visualization. I've previously worked at NVIDIA, Stanford AI Lab, Stanford NLP Group, and Mayo Clinic.

I'm originally from Rochester, Minnesota, where I shoveled snow, ice-skated, and admired the two hours of yearly sunshine. I'm a proud alum of Century High School and the now-closed Friedell Middle School 😢. In 2020, I was one of two Minnesotans to be named a U.S. Presidential Scholar.

Outside of work, I enjoy romance & young adult fiction, Pixar movies, hiking, moonlighting as a Marvel Phase 4 evangelist, and pretending that I'm a Jedi.
